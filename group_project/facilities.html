FACILITIES

Our amenities, which include a variety of lodging options from comfortable dorms to private rooms, are created with your comfort and convenience in mind. Take use of our spacious parking area, clean facilities, and high-speed Wi-Fi to stay connected. Eco-friendly procedures demonstrate our dedication to sustainability and guarantee that you can explore responsibly. Our Mahallah is not simply a place to stay but a center of comfort, connectedness, and community with laundry facilities and a committed committee eager to make your stay unforgettable.

CAFE

Our cafe is famous for having variety choices of foods such as shawarma, fajitas, mi tarik and many more.

COURTS
